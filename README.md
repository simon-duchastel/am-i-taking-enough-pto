# Am I taking enough PTO?

If you have a job with unlimited PTO, it can be hard to know whether you're taking enough or just working yourself to death. [Many people with unlimited PTO don't take enough](https://namely.com/blog/does-your-pto-plan-type-even-really-matter) (although the numbers are hard to find).

If you're not sure whether you're taking enough PTO, use this app to track your usage and remind yourself to take enough PTO!
